
- unittests verifies a specific aspect of a functions behaviours
- test case is the range of behaviours covered, full coverage is aimed to be
  achieved after deployment
- testing a class ensures that the behaviour of the class is working well

assertEqual(a, b) #Verify that a == b
assertNotEqual(a, b) #Verify that a != b
assertTrue(x) #Verify that x is True
assertFalse(x) #Verify that x is False
assertIn(item, list) #Verify that item is in list
assertNotIn(item, list) #Verify that item is not in list
